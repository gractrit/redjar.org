Day Three

Clinton, NJ - Martinsburg, WV
Date: 06/13/2002
Distance: 286 miles
Weather: Cloudy with occasional rain. Mid 70's.

Today we left the Tierney house after a waffle breakfast. Heading into Pennsylvania, our first stop was Reading. However, when we arrived in Reading we just kept on driving. Some nice houses, but not much else to do or see.

Next we headed to lunch at the Cloister Restaurant but not the Cloister. Driving through Lancaster, Intercourse, and Gettysburg, we actually make it to West Viginia for the night. Rainy weather made use wary of camping (we don't have a tarp yet) so we looked for a hotel or hostel for the night. After several failed attempts, we ended up at the Pikeside Motel for the night (shhhh... quiet Ogden!).
