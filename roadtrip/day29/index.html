Day Twenty-Nine

Aloha, OR - Mossyrock, WA
Date: 07/09/2002
Distance: 108 miles
Weather: Mostly Sunny 75-95

Today it was nice to sleep in a little late. After a hot shower (no coins required), the three of us headed to OMSI- Oregon Museum of Science and Industry. There we played with science toys of all kinds, and Kim and Corin predicted the weather. There was even time to see an OMNI-IMAX show called Space Station.

After playing the morning away, we were ready to set out again. We said goodbye to Kim, grabbed lunch at a local market, and drove right into Washington State (ouch). We stopped at a state park in Mossyrock, WA, a town with many hidden charms (we guessed), we ventured into, which was nearly deserted. We had dinner at the Viking Cafe, basically a buger joint. We returned home, played one card game of spit and went to sleep.
